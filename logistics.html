Logistics

NVOCC (Non Vessel Operating Common Carriers

We represent many NVOCC operators from the South-East Asia and Indian subcontinent. Through our dedicated facility FreeZone we are in a position to rework the shipments and arrange for on-forwarding to other Gulf ports at competitive rates. Our agents in China, Indian subcontinent, and Europe send us group-age containers on a weekly basic besides FCLs and other co-loaded LCLs. We act as agents on behalf of our overseas principles.

Supply Chain Management and Solutions

Global Freight Forwarding and Networks: Our International network of agents and partners elp our customers to get their products to their market on time and cost effectively and cutting through complex documentation and customs compliance requirements at both origin and destination, which requires experienced experts with the knowledge and global reach. Our Freight forwarding team offers integrated supply chain solutions that leverage our network of offices worldwide and serve a broad customer base with a full range of multi level transportation requirements by air, ocean, rail, barge or land.

Project Forwarding and Logistics

MRC Shipping & Logistics together with our Project partners Worldwide provide suplly chain management solutions both locally and internationally. We have also entered into project forwarding with the support of our partners and now handle project shipments including the rebuild of various war damaged countries including Iraq & Afganistan and have delivered heavy lifts and machieneries for various energy and water supply project in and out of the region. We have also undertaken turnkey projects for various upcoming projects and developments within the region itself. These include procurement, collection from the suppliers factory, freight by rail, road, ocean and air, storage and transshipments.

Third Party Logistics and Warehousing

Our Facilities include state of art warehouses that are capable of meeting any storage requirements including dry and temp controlled. Bonded Stoarage & Re-export / Re-distribution Transportation and Distribution network

LCL Services

Our NVOCC LCL operations in Jabel Ali Free Trade Zone handle consolidation (import & export) for various operations, both domestic & international, from india subcontinent, South East Asia, Arabian Gulf Eroup & USA. As a part of our ongoing development programs, we are working on further expansion of our activities and developing business relationships with new partners and agents worldwide.

Packing and Removals

MRC Shipping & Logistics caters for personalize service by clearing and exporting of personal effects. Experienced Staff carries oot a survey of your personal belongings and with the help of experienced professional packers, ensures that your personal effects are packed with the right packing material with utmost care, labelled correctly to ensure that no damage occures during local and sea-air transportaion. All the hassles of booking the earliest mode of transportation, preparing the required documents for exports and processing the export documents are all done with utmost speed and accuracy, thereby ensuring that your goods reach the destination in perfect order and good condition.
