LAND TRANSPORTATION

Our Land Transport Management Team provides regular LTL/FTL services within the GCC countries. Our flexible and tailor made road operations ensure to meet the demands of our customers.

The Services Offered:

  • Container transportation
  • Land transportation to GCC countries (LTL and FTL)
  • Break bulk movements
  • Special and heavy equipment movements
  • Flexible and timely arranging of cranes and forklifts
  • Fleet of own trucks with 40 Vehicles
  • In house customs clearance
  • Door - to - door clearance
  • Insurance Services
  • Specialized Hazardous I Perishable material Handling

OCEAN FREIGHT

Our Ocean Freight Management Team handles movement of cargoes from any global origin/destination, be it LCL or FCL with strategic, and with our stable relationships with leading carriers, thereby ensuring competitive rates and excellent services.

The Services Offered:

  • Sea Freight Import I Export Consolidation - LCL and FCL services
  • Document Delivery
  • Logistics & distribution services
  • Quality process and services for receiving, delivering, bonded storage, forwarding shipping and consolidation, de-consolidation, packing, lashing and crating
  • Dedicated Customs Clearance for Import & Export Shipments
  • Hassle free service for all types of customs documentation and delivery
  • Specialized Hazardous I Perishable material Handling

AIR FREIGHT

Our Air Freight Management Team offers sophisticated and innovative services. Our expertise on air import and export requirements, customs regulation, licenses or approvals, consular doucumentation and payment practices guide our customers towards favorable air freight opreations.

The Services Offered:

  • Air Freight (Export I Import Consolidation)
  • Projects Cargo Handling
  • Aircraft Chartering
  • Dedicated Customs Clearance for Import & Export shipment
  • Door to Door Service
  • Insurance Services
  • Specialized Hazardous I Perishable material Handling

WARE HOUSING

We offer a full range of warehousing service with experienced employees as well as on- site nagement. We believe that warehouse services, especially the loading and unloading process, should to be left to question or chance. Our primary focus is to raise these services to the same efficient and reliable level.

The Services Offered:

  • Inventory : management & control
  • Packing & removal, labeling, quality inspection, palletizing & other value added services
  • Secured open & covered storage facility.
  • Professional & well experienced supply chain team committed to optimize time and cost of deliveries
  • Trusted & reliable partner with ability to customize quickly.
